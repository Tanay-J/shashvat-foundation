Our Vision

Shashvat Paediatric Care Foundation is a volunteer-driven organization where every donation directly supports pediatric cancer patients. Our volunteers manage all tasks independently, ensuring no additional financial burden is placed on the Trust. We operate with zero overhead costs, allowing 100% of the donations to go directly to patient care.

We began our journey in collaboration with the National Cancer Institute, Nagpur, where we provided financial support to pediatric patients from underprivileged families. The Social Work Department identifies the needy patients, and we pay directly to the hospital to cover their treatment costs. To date, we are proud to have supported 8 patients and their families in accessing cancer treatment.

We are also fortunate to have partnered with Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai, where we currently support 12 pediatric patients with their diagnostic expenses. On February 6, 2022, we sponsored the cancer treatment for 2 additional children.

Recognizing that some families struggle even for basic necessities, we extend our support to cover lodging and boarding for these families at Shri Gadge Baba Trust in Sion, Mumbai, which is conveniently located near Tata Memorial Hospital.

Our Programs

Distribution of Gifts in Festive Seasons

Our primary goal is to bring smiles to the faces of our little champions. We provide gift hampers & school bags to children undergoing treatment in hospitals, especially during festive seasons, spreading joy and creating more happy moments for the kids.

Fruits & Veggies Sunday

On Sundays, we distribute fresh fruits and vegetables to patients and orphaned children. Join us to witness the impact of a passionate team dedicated to making a difference.

Patient Lodging & Boarding

Through Shri Gadge Baba Trust in Sion (W), Mumbai, we assist patients with affordable lodging and boarding. Conveniently located close to Tata Memorial Hospital, this arrangement provides essential support for patients and their families.
