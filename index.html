About Us

Shashvat Paediatric Care Foundation is a voluntary organization dedicated to making a difference in the lives of children battling cancer. Our commitment is unwavering: every penny donated goes directly to the patients we serve.

Our sole purpose is to support paediatric cancer patients from underprivileged families. We provide comprehensive support, covering pre-diagnostic expenses, cancer treatment costs, and lodging & boarding expenses. Our goal is to ensure that no child is denied the chance to fight cancer due to financial constraints.

Learn More About Our Mission

Team

Our team is made up of a small group of dedicated individuals who are passionate about making a difference in the lives of children fighting cancer and ensure that children receive the care they need.

History

We started our association with National Cancer Institute, Nagpur and started providing financial support to the paediatric patients coming from under privileged families.

Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every child diagnosed with cancer receives the highest quality of care, regardless of their financial situation. We aim to create a holistic support system for children and their families

Difference We Are Making

At Shashvat Paediatric Care Foundation, we are committed to changing the lives of children battling cancer. Through early diagnosis, comprehensive treatment, and emotional support, we empower families to fight this challenging journey with hope. Our dedicated team ensures access to cutting-edge medical care while addressing the unique needs of each child. By partnering with hospitals, caregivers, and communities, we strive to reduce the financial and emotional burden of cancer treatment, offering a brighter future for every young patient.
